Buying vegan products sometimes can be tricky.  
Animal ingredients can be hiding in the details of your clothing without your knowlege.  
Always check those details: 

- Buttons 
- Labels 
- Zipper  
- Linners 
- Insoles 
- Cases 
- Toggle
